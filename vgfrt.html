Specifications

Intel Core i9 Ultra

  • Architecture: 10nm SuperFin
  • Cores: 16
  • Threads: 32
  • Base Clock: 3.0 GHz
  • Boost Clock: Up to 5.0 GHz
  • Thermal Design Power (TDP): 125W

NVIDIA RTX 5090

  • CUDA Cores: 16,384
  • Memory: 24GB GDDR7
  • Ray Tracing Cores: 3rd Generation
  • Tensor Cores: 4th Generation
  • DLSS Support: Yes

Performance Benchmarks

The Intel Core i9 Ultra and NVIDIA RTX 5090 deliver exceptional performance in gaming and content creation.

  • Gaming Performance: Up to 20% faster than the previous generation.
  • Content Creation: Significant improvements in rendering and editing times.
  • Power Efficiency: Better thermal management and reduced power consumption.
